How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your The Doer account and all associated data using one of the following methods:

1

In-App Deletion (Recommended)

Open The Doer app > Go to Profile > Settings > Delete Account > Confirm deletion

2

Email Request

Send an email to privacy@thedoer.app with the subject "Delete My Account" and include your registered email address

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days:

Data Type Status
Profile information (name, email, photo) Deleted
Fitness activity data (steps, runs, workouts) Deleted
Challenge participation and progress Deleted
Group memberships and messages Deleted
Location and route history Deleted
Badges and achievements Deleted
App preferences and settings Deleted

Data Retention

Some data may be retained for a limited period after account deletion:

Data Type Retention Period Reason
Transaction records 7 years Legal/tax requirements
Anonymized analytics Indefinite Cannot be linked to you
Support ticket history 90 days Quality assurance

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. You will lose all your fitness history, achievements, and challenge progress. If you rejoin The Doer, you will start with a fresh account.

Request Account Deletion

Send your deletion request to:

privacy@thedoer.app
Subject: Delete My Account

Hi,

I would like to delete my The Doer account.

Registered email: [your email]

Thank you.

We will process your request within 30 days and send confirmation to your email.
